Things to Do in Tangub

Tangub Plaza

Location: Maloro, Tangub City

Tangub in Misamis Occidental doesn't let Christmas pass without so much fanfare. They take decorating for the holidays seriously that the word simple doesn't even exist. In fact, they hold the title as the Christmas Symbols Capital of the Philippines.

Hoyohoy View Deck

Location: P-6 HOYOHOY, Tangub City, 7214 Misamis Occidental

Amazing view of Panguil Bay Northern Mindanao. Beautiful landscape of Tangub mountains. It has a pretty cool observation deck. It’s nice to go up there anytime of the day it has a cooler temperature. I love that they have those little stores where you buy grilled plantains on a stick and waters. I hired a van for the whole family to get up there. Entrance fees was 30 peso per person. We really enjoyed our family trip. I would recommend this place and I will definitely come back.

Hoyohoy Highland Stone Chapel

Location: P-5 HOYOHOY, Tangub City, 7214 Misamis Occidental

Hoyohoy Highland Stone Chapel Hotel and Adventure Park is owned and operated by Highland Stone Chapel Corp. Our flagship extreme recreation is the Sky-Line Zip which is considered the Highest and Longest in Asia. (Two lines: First Line: 1.250 Kms and Second Line: 1.100 Kms Total of 2.350 Kms)started last July 16, 2010 then two years after we operated our Hotel. We are located at the foot of Mt. Malindang Mountain Ranges which is around 3,000 feet above sea-level over looking Lanao del Norte, Tangub City, and Ozamiz City and most importantly the majestic view of sunrise and Panguil Bay. Our Aspiration is to be the Top Tourist and Seminar Destination in whole Mindanao.

Museo Handurawan

Location: Sinanduloy Cultural Center, Tangub City, Misamis Occidental

Department: City Mayor`s Office

Section: City Information/Documentation Office/Tangub City Historical Commission

Location: Tangub City Activity Center behind City Café II

Contact Number: 09262330080

ABOUT THE SERVICE:

The City Mayor's Office allows clients to view the historical and cultural collections housed at Museo Handurawan under the management of Tangub City Historical Commission. The viewing gives the clients glimpse and understanding of the early Tangubanons` way of life that will enable them to appreciate and value the city’s rich cultural heritage.
